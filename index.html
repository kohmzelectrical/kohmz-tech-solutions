Technical Advisory (FAQ)

My house is older. How do I know if I need a panel upgrade?

Signs include tripping breakers, flickering lights, or warm panel covers. We perform load evaluations and Digital Insulation Resistance Testing to ensure your safety.

Do you handle dedicated wiring for new AC units?

Yes. We provide dedicated wiring, load evaluations, and proper breakers to ensure your cooling system operates safely without overloading existing circuits.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or sparks?

Stop using it immediately and turn off the breaker. Our team provides fast technical diagnostics to identify faulty wiring or potential system failure risks.

Can you design the electrical layout for a new commercial space?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom wiring, CAD drawings, lighting design, and NEMA-compliant enclosures for professional commercial fit-outs.

How can regular maintenance benefit my business?

Preventative technical maintenance avoids unexpected failures and operational downtime through systematic diagnostics and insulation testing.

Can you install heavy-duty power systems?

Yes, we implement entrance wiring, service meter bases, and commercial-grade outlets designed for high-capacity industrial electrical loads.
