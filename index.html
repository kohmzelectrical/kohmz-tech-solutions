Technical Advisory (FAQ)

Why is a panel upgrade better than just adding breakers?

A panel upgrade ensures that your main distribution board can handle the total cumulative load of your property. Adding breakers to an undersized panel is the leading cause of "panel fires." We calculate your service entrance capacity before any upgrades.

Does KOHMZ provide technical documentation for commercial permits?

Absolutely. We specialize in providing CAD-driven electrical designs, load calculations, and single-line diagrams required for professional safety compliance and business permit inspections.

What is the 'KOHMZ Advantage' in Solar implementation?

We focus on the "Electrical Backbone" of your solar setup. We ensure that your Hybrid or Off-Grid system is integrated seamlessly with your main grid using certified ATS (Automatic Transfer Switch) and DC protection suites.
