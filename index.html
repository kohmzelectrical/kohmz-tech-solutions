Technical Advisory FAQ

Common questions regarding power safety, code compliance, and system upgrades.

1. Is my property at risk of an electrical fire?

If you experience warm switch plates, frequent breaker tripping, or flickering lights, an immediate audit is required. These are clear indicators of overloaded circuits or micro-arcing inside the walls.

2. Do you handle AC installation wiring?

Yes, we install dedicated load centers specifically for high-amperage HVAC networks to ensure they do not interfere with your general lighting and convenience outlets.

3. What is PEC Compliance?

It is the strict adherence to the Philippine Electrical Code. Non-compliance not only risks lives but can also void your fire insurance policies in the event of an incident.

4. What to do if an outlet sparks?

Stop using it immediately and turn off the corresponding breaker at your main panel. Do not attempt to plug anything back in until a licensed technician has verified the terminal connections.

5. What is the Solar Integration process?

It strictly begins with a Load Audit to determine the right inverter and battery capacity for your grid. We do not guess sizes; we compute them based on your actual daily kilowatt-hour (kWh) consumption.

6. How long does a Technical Audit take?

A standard residential or commercial audit usually takes between 1 to 2 hours, depending on the size of the property and the complexity of the existing wiring infrastructure.

7. Do you provide as-built electrical plans?

Yes. Upon project completion, we provide comprehensive as-built CAD plans, signed and sealed by a Professional Electrical Engineer (PEE), ready for LGU or Meralco requirements.

8. Why is my breaker frequently tripping?

A tripping breaker is doing its job—protecting you from an overload, a short circuit, or a ground fault. If it happens frequently, the circuit needs an immediate thermal scan and load reassessment.

9. Can I upgrade my main panel without rewiring the whole house?

Yes, provided that the existing home-run wires pass our rigorous Digital Insulation Resistance tests. If the old wires are brittle or degraded, partial or full rewiring will be legally mandated.

10. Are your solar setups Net-Metering ready?

Absolutely. We design and install bi-directional compatible systems that meet all exact technical standards required by your LGU and your local power distributor (e.g., Meralco) for seamless net-metering enrollment.
