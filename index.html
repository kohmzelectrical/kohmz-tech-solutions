Technical Advisory (FAQ)

Is my property at risk? When should I upgrade my panel?

If you experience warm panel covers, flickering lights, or frequent breaker trips, your system is overloaded. We perform Digital Insulation Resistance Testing to detect internal faults before they become fire hazards.

Do you handle dedicated technical wiring for new AC units?

Yes. We provide certified AC load centers and dedicated wiring to ensure your cooling system operates safely without stressing your existing infrastructure.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or sparks?

Stop using it immediately and turn off the circuit. Our team provides rapid technical diagnostics to identify faulty wiring or potential system failure risks before they escalate.

Can you design the electrical layout for a new commercial fit-out?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom wiring architecture, CAD designs, and NEMA-compliant enclosures required for professional safety permits and business compliance.

How can regular technical maintenance benefit my business?

Preventative diagnostics avoid unexpected operational downtime and catastrophic equipment failures through systematic diagnostics and insulation testing.

Can you install heavy-duty industrial power systems?

Yes, we implement entrance wiring, service meter bases, and commercial-grade outlets designed for high-capacity industrial and retail electrical loads.
