Frequently Asked Questions

My house is older. How do I know if I need a panel upgrade or rewiring?

Signs include tripping breakers, flickering lights, or the need to use multiple extension cords. We can perform a full load evaluation and insulation resistance testing to determine if your panel box needs upgrading.

Do you handle dedicated wiring for new air conditioning (AC) units?

Yes. Air conditioners draw significant power. We provide dedicated AC wiring, load evaluations, and proper circuit breakers to ensure your cooling system operates safely without overloading infrastructure.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or sparks?

Stop using it immediately and turn off the corresponding circuit breaker. Our team provides fast diagnostics to identify whether the issue is a faulty outlet, loose entrance wiring, or a larger failure.
