Technical Advisory (FAQ)

Is my property at risk? When should I upgrade my panel?

Tripping breakers, flickering lights, or warm panel covers are critical red flags. We perform Digital Insulation Resistance Testing to detect internal faults before they become fire hazards.

Do you handle dedicated technical wiring for new AC units?

Yes. We provide certified AC load centers and dedicated wiring to ensure your cooling system operates safely without stressing existing infrastructure.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or sparks?

Stop using it immediately and turn off the circuit. Our team provides rapid technical diagnostics to identify faulty wiring or potential system failure risks.

Can you design the electrical layout for a new commercial space?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom wiring architecture, CAD-driven designs, and NEMA-compliant enclosures required for professional fit-outs.

How can regular technical maintenance benefit my business?

Preventative diagnostics avoid unexpected operational downtime and equipment failures through systematic diagnostics and insulation testing.

Can you install heavy-duty industrial power systems?

Yes, we implement entrance wiring, service meter bases, and commercial-grade outlets designed for high-capacity industrial and retail electrical loads.
