Frequently Asked Questions

My house is older. How do I know if I need a panel upgrade?

Signs include tripping breakers, flickering lights, or multiple extension cords. We perform load evaluations and insulation resistance testing.

Do you handle dedicated wiring for new AC units?

Yes. We provide dedicated AC wiring, load evaluations, and proper breakers to ensure your cooling system operates safely.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or sparks?

Stop using it immediately and turn off the breaker. Our team provides fast diagnostics to identify faulty wiring or system failure.

Can you design the electrical layout for a new commercial space?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom wiring, CAD drawings, lighting design, and NEMA enclosures for commercial fit-outs.

How can regular maintenance benefit my business?

Preventative maintenance avoids unexpected failures and downtime through system diagnostics and insulation testing.

Can you install heavy-duty power systems?

Yes, we install entrance wiring, service meter bases, and commercial-grade outlets for higher electrical loads.
