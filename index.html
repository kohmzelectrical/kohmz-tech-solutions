Frequently Asked Questions

My house is older. How do I know if I need a panel upgrade or rewiring?

Signs that you may need an upgrade include frequently tripping circuit breakers, flickering lights, or the need to use multiple extension cords. We can perform a full load evaluation and insulation resistance testing to determine if your main or sub-panel boxes need upgrading to safely meet your family's power demands.

Do you handle dedicated wiring for new air conditioning (AC) units?

Yes. Air conditioners draw a significant amount of power. We provide dedicated AC wiring, load evaluations, and the installation of proper circuit breakers to ensure your new cooling system operates safely without overloading your home's existing electrical infrastructure.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or sparks?

Stop using the outlet immediately and turn off the corresponding circuit breaker. Our team provides fast diagnostics and repairs to identify whether the issue is a faulty power outlet, loose entrance wiring, or a larger system failure, ensuring your home remains safe from fire hazards.

Can you design the electrical layout for a new commercial space or office fit-out?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom wiring designs for commercial spaces. We provide professional CAD electrical drawings, lighting system design, and the installation of electrical gutters and NEMA enclosures to ensure your new space is both functional and fully code-compliant.

How can regular electrical maintenance benefit my business?

Preventative maintenance is crucial for businesses to avoid unexpected power failures that cause costly downtime. We provide complete system diagnostics, insulation resistance testing, and load evaluations to identify and resolve potential issues before they disrupt your daily operations.

Can you install heavy-duty power systems for commercial equipment?

Yes, we install complete entrance wiring and conduits, service meter bases, and commercial-grade power outlets designed to handle higher electrical loads safely and efficiently.
