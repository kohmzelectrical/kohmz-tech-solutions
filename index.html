Technical Advisory (FAQ)

Expert answers to the most critical electrical concerns for property owners, architects, and facility managers.

Is my property at risk? When should I upgrade my electrical panel?

Tripping breakers, flickering lights, a slight burning smell, o warm panel covers ay critical red flags indicating a severe amperage deficit. Modern smart appliances draw continuous loads na hindi kaya ng mga lumang panels. Nagsasagawa kami ng comprehensive load evaluations para makita ang internal faults bago sila maging fire hazards.

Do you handle dedicated technical wiring for new heavy AC units?

Yes. Ang simpleng pag-tap ng bagong high-HP air conditioner sa existing line ay lubhang mapanganib dahil sa massive compressor inrush currents. Nagbibigay kami ng certified AC load centers at dedicated home-run circuits directly mula sa main panel para mapanatiling ligtas ang iyong cooling system.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or emits sparks?

Huwag gamitin ang outlet immediately at i-turn off ang corresponding breaker. Ang sparking ay nagpapahiwatig ng loose terminal arcing, na isang immediate fire hazard na nakatago sa iyong mga pader. Ang aming koponan ay nagbibigay ng mabilis na technical diagnostics para palitan ang compromised materials safely.

Can you design the electrical layout for a new commercial space?

Absolutely. Nagpapakadalubhasa kami sa custom wiring architecture, scalable power grids para sa workstation ergonomics, at NEMA-compliant enclosures. Ang aming CAD documents ay meticulously prepared para makapasa sa rigorous LGU safety permits at commercial building inspections.

How can regular technical maintenance benefit my business?

Ang proactive maintenance ay exponentially cheaper kaysa sa disaster recovery. Ang preventative diagnostics ay naiiwasan ang unexpected operational downtime, pinoprotektahan ang sensitive corporate servers mula sa power surges, at pinipigilan ang equipment failures.

Can you install heavy-duty industrial power systems?

Yes, fully equipped kami para mag-implement ng heavy entrance wiring, 3-phase service meter bases, at commercial-grade distribution panels specifically structured para sa maximum operational endurance sa high-capacity industrial environments.
