Technical Advisory (FAQ)

Expert answers to the most critical electrical concerns for property owners and facility managers.

Is my property at risk? When should I upgrade my electrical panel?

Tripping breakers, flickering lights, a slight burning smell, or warm panel covers are critical red flags indicating a severe amperage deficit and thermal degradation. Modern smart appliances draw continuous loads that old panels cannot handle. We perform comprehensive load evaluations and Digital Insulation Resistance Testing to detect internal faults before they escalate into structural fire hazards.

Do you handle dedicated technical wiring for new heavy AC units?

Yes. Simply tapping a new high-HP air conditioner into an existing line is highly dangerous due to massive compressor inrush currents. We provide certified AC load centers, dedicated home-run circuits directly from the main panel, and exact breaker sizing to ensure your cooling system operates safely while preventing voltage drops that destroy expensive compressor motors.

What should I do if an outlet stops working or emits sparks?

Stop using the outlet immediately and turn off the corresponding breaker at your main panel. Sparking indicates loose terminal arcing or oxidized contacts, which is an immediate fire hazard hidden inside your walls. Our team provides rapid technical diagnostics to isolate the faulty wiring and replace the compromised materials safely.

Can you design the electrical layout for a new commercial space?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom wiring architecture, three-phase distribution, scalable power grids for workstation ergonomics, and NEMA-compliant enclosures. Our CAD documents are meticulously prepared to pass rigorous LGU safety permits, fire department compliance checks, and commercial building inspections without delay.

How can regular technical maintenance benefit my business?

Proactive maintenance is exponentially cheaper than disaster recovery. Preventative diagnostics avoid unexpected operational downtime, protect sensitive corporate servers from power surges, and prevent catastrophic equipment failures by identifying weak points in your system before they physically fail.

Can you install heavy-duty industrial power systems?

Yes, we are fully equipped to implement heavy entrance wiring, 3-phase service meter bases, motor control centers, heavy-duty raceways, and commercial-grade distribution panels specifically engineered for maximum operational endurance in high-capacity industrial and manufacturing environments.
