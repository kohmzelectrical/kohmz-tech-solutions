Electrical faults are the #1 cause of structural fires. Do not wait for sparks or burning odors before consulting an expert.

  • 2. Appliance Damage
    Unstable voltage and power surges can permanently fry expensive appliances.
  • 3. Power Leakage
    Faulty wiring and grounded lines mean you are paying for electricity you are not even using.

    Exposed wires or improperly grounded systems pose a direct electrocution hazard.
