Safe, standard-compliant, and reliable residential electrical installations to protect your home and your family.

Powering Your Dream Home

From new house construction wiring to fixing a stubborn tripped breaker, KOHMZ Electrical brings industrial-level precision into your home. We ensure your electrical system can handle today’s modern appliances safely.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Older homes often cannot handle the load of modern air conditioners and heaters. We upgrade service panels and meter bases to prevent fire hazards and ensure smooth power flow.

Dedicated Appliance Wiring

Installing a new Air Conditioner or Water Heater? We provide dedicated circuits with proper breaker sizing so your appliances don't overload your home's main system.

24/7 Troubleshooting & Repair

Sparking outlets? Flickering lights? Power outages in one room? Stop using the affected area immediately and call our emergency response team for rapid diagnostics and repair.

Why Choose KOHMZ for Your Home?

We treat your home with the utmost respect. We utilize proper wire management, high-grade THHN/THWN copper wires, and perform rigorous safety testing before we leave any residential project.
