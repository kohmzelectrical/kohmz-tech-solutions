Comprehensive Commercial Services

Downtime means lost revenue. At KOHMZ Electrical Design and Build, we provide robust, high-capacity electrical infrastructure tailored for commercial spaces, from retail stores to large office buildings.

Office Fit-outs & Custom Wiring

Complete electrical layout designing using CAD. We handle lighting systems, workstation power distribution, and server room dedicated circuits to ensure your office operates at peak efficiency.

Load Evaluation & Balancing

Prevent system overloads. We perform meticulous load calculations before adding new heavy equipment, ensuring absolute compliance with the Philippine Electrical Code (PEC).

Heavy-Duty Panel Upgrades

Modernize your building's electrical core. We install NEMA-compliant enclosures, 3-phase systems, and execute sub-panel integrations for better energy management.
