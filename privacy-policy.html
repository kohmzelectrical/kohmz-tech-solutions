Corporate Policies & Terms_

Our commitment to data privacy and professional operational standards.

At KOHMZ Electrical Design and Build, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. By using our website and submitting inquiries, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection

We collect necessary personal information when you use our "Request a Quote" or "Book Now" forms. This includes your Full Name, Email Address, Contact Number, Property Address, and project-related photos or blueprints.

2. Use of Collected Data

Your information is used strictly for operational purposes: to provide accurate technical assessments, issue formal quotations, schedule site inspections, and communicate updates regarding your requested electrical or solar projects.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement strict security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All project blueprints, layout photos, and personal details submitted are kept highly confidential and stored securely within our technical database.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We absolutely do not sell, trade, or rent your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data is only shared with trusted internal engineers and specialists directly involved in executing your project.

5. Client Rights & Control

You have the right to request the deletion or modification of your personal data and project files from our system at any time after the completion of the assessment or project. Simply contact our support team to initiate the request.

By engaging the services of KOHMZ Electrical Design and Build, you agree to be bound by the following professional terms and operational guidelines:

1. Quotations & Estimates

All initial quotes provided via email or messaging are estimates based on the information provided by the client. Final project costs are subject to change and will only be locked in following a comprehensive, on-site technical inspection by our specialists.

2. Safety & Code Compliance

KOHMZ Electrical refuses to compromise on structural safety. All electrical, wiring, and solar installations are executed in strict compliance with the Philippine Electrical Code (PEC) and NEMA standards.

3. Project Agreements & Payment

For mid-to-large-scale operations, payment milestones and specific deliverables will be outlined in a formal contract. This agreement must be signed and the initial mobilization fee settled before the commencement of any physical work.

4. Warranty & Workmanship

We stand by the quality of our execution. KOHMZ provides a formal workmanship warranty for all new installations and panel upgrades. Warranties are voided if the system is tampered with by unauthorized third-party technicians.

5. Liability & Project Delays

While we strive for strict adherence to timelines, KOHMZ Electrical is not liable for project delays caused by unforeseen circumstances such as extreme weather or delays in third-party utility approvals.

Due to the customized nature of electrical engineering and procurement, our refund and cancellation structures are strictly defined:

1. Initial Mobilization Fees

Once a project contract is signed and the initial downpayment/mobilization fee is deposited, it becomes non-refundable. This fee immediately covers the logistics, blueprint drafting, and pre-purchasing of authentic copper wires, breakers, and materials specific to your layout.

2. Project Cancellation by Client

If a client decides to halt a project mid-execution, they will be billed for all the labor hours rendered and materials installed up to that exact date. Remaining materials stored on-site that are already paid for will be turned over to the client.

3. Service Rescheduling

We understand that construction schedules change. Clients must notify us at least 48 hours in advance if an agreed-upon deployment date needs to be moved. Repeated sudden cancellations may incur a minimal rescheduling penalty due to the lost hours of our dispatched engineers.
