About KOHMZ Electrical Design and Build

KOHMZ Electrical Design and Build is a professional contracting firm dedicated to providing safe, efficient, and high-quality solutions. We specialize in comprehensive technical design, certified installation, and professional maintenance for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.
