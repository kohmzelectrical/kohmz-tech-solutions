Smart Solar Solutions: The Future of Energy Independence

Investing in Solar is the single most effective way to eliminate rising electricity costs. At KOHMZ, we use only Tier-1 equipment to guarantee 25+ years of performance. Here is how we pick the right system for you:

1. Grid-Tied System

"Best for Maximum ROI"

  • Lowers daytime bills by up to 80%.
  • Directly feeds excess power back to the grid.
  • Simple, low maintenance, and fastest payback.

2. Off-Grid System

"Best for Remote Areas"

  • 100% independence from utility companies.
  • Relies on heavy-duty battery storage.
  • Perfect for rest houses or areas with frequent blackouts.
PRO'S TOP PICK

3. Hybrid System

"Best for Modern PH Homes"

  • BILL REDUCTION + BACKUP POWER.
  • Seamlessly switches to batteries during brownouts.
  • The ultimate balance of savings and reliability.

"Our Recommendation: For most Metro Manila homes, a **Hybrid System** is the best pick. It protects your comfort during outages while slashing your Meralco bills simultaneously."
