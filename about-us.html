We are a specialist technical firm dedicated to high-standard electrical architecture, absolute compliance, and advanced renewable energy integration. We don't just wire buildings; we program power environments.

The Design-First Philosophy

At KOHMZ Electrical Design and Build, we believe that world-class electrical systems are engineered before a single wire is pulled. Born from the necessity to elevate Philippine electrical standards, our firm bridges the gap between conventional contracting and cutting-edge technical architecture.

Our approach eliminates guesswork. Through rigorous CAD-based layouts, precise load calculations, and adherence to the Philippine Electrical Code (PEC), we curate infrastructures that are not only safe today but are fully capable of adapting to the technological demands of tomorrow.

Infrastructure Expertise

Residential Protocols

We transform ordinary houses into "Smart & Safe" havens. From dedicated circuits for heavy appliances to high-grade GFCI/AFCI installations, we ensure your family’s absolute protection against electrical fire hazards.

Commercial Systems

Downtime is not an option. We specialize in robust 3-phase power distributions, custom workstation fit-outs, and preventive maintenance designed to keep your business operations running 24/7 without interruption.

Renewable Integration

We lead the transition to energy independence. By deploying Tier-1 Solar components and Hybrid inverter systems, we provide sustainable solutions that slash utility costs and protect against grid blackouts.

The KOHMZ Quality Guarantee

Trust is built on transparency and technical execution. When you hire KOHMZ, you are partnering with specialists who utilize industrial-grade tools—from Megohmmeter insulation testing to precise voltage diagnostics—guaranteeing that every connection is flawless.

We procure only authentic, high-grade materials (THHN/THWN copper, NEMA-rated enclosures) to ensure your system’s longevity. We don't cut corners; we set the standard.
