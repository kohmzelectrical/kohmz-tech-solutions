Our Technical Execution Framework

We eliminate "trial and error." Every project begins with professional CAD electrical drawings. This allows us to map out the most efficient wire routes and identify potential circuit bottlenecks.

Our specialists perform deep load evaluations to match your property's consumption with the correct breaker and wire sizing. We provide transparent material and labor cost estimates.

Implementation is handled by certified specialists utilizing industrial-grade conduits, proper wire management systems, and NEMA-compliant enclosures.

The "KOHMZ Standard" requires rigorous final testing. We utilize Digital Megohmmeters for insulation resistance testing to detect hidden micro-faults.
